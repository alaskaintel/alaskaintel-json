# 10: Revenue & Sustainability

## Overview
Establishing a sustainable financial model for AlaskaIntel that aligns with its mission of providing public-interest intelligence.

## Vision
AlaskaIntel should be a self-sustaining utility for the state, funded by ethical advertising and premium data services.

## Planned Features
- **Alaska Ad Engine**: A custom, high-intent ad network for local businesses that triggers based on signals (e.g., "Fishing season starts" -> "Buy boots at Big Ray's").
- **Premium Data API**: Providing high-frequency or bulk data access to businesses, research institutions, and government agencies.
- **Micro-Sponsorships**: Allowing users to "Sponsor a Signal Feed" to ensure its continued maintenance.
- **Public Infrastructure Grants**: Seeking alignment with state and federal digital infrastructure initiatives.
