# 05: Alaska Native Intelligence

## Overview
Deeply integrating the voices and decisions of Alaska's 200+ Tribes and 13 Regional Corporations into the AlaskaIntel Signal Wall.

## Vision
Indigenous sovereignty and corporate governance are central to Alaska's future. These signals must be first-class citizens in the Alaska OS.

## Planned Features
- **ANCSA Portal Monitoring**: Scraping press releases and land-use notices from Regional and Village corporations.
- **Tribal News Aggregation**: Consolidating news from the Alaska Federation of Natives (AFN), Tanana Chiefs Conference, and Sitka Tribe.
- **Cultural Calendar**: A unified feed of Potlatches, Festivals (Cama-i, Kivgiq), and Subsistence updates.
- **Native Language Integration**: Supporting signals in Inupiaq, Yup'ik, and Tlingit where available.
