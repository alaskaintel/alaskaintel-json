# 06: Economic Pulse Dashboard

## Overview
A real-time dashboard for tracking the "vital signs" of the Alaska economy, providing context for the signals on the Wall.

## Vision
Every Alaskan should know the current "State of the State" in one glance.

## Planned Features
- **Resource Price Ticker**: Real-time ANS Crude, Gold, Copper, and Salmon market prices.
- **State Revenue Tracker**: Automated scraping of Alaska Dept of Revenue throughput and fiscal reports.
- **Employment Data**: Integrating monthly DOLWD signals for regional employment shifts.
- **PFD Predictions**: Sentiment and fiscal analysis of legislative debates regarding the Permanent Fund Dividend.
